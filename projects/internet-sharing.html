Design for Internet Sharing App

A peer-to-peer internet sharing platform that enables users to monetize their unused bandwidth while providing affordable connectivity to those who need it most.

Mobile Design UX Research User Testing
Client ConnectShare
Timeline 14 Weeks
Year 2024

The Vision

In a world where internet access is increasingly essential, millions still struggle with connectivity. ConnectShare envisioned a decentralized solution: let people with excess bandwidth share it with neighbors, travelers, or anyone in need—creating a community-powered internet ecosystem.

Our challenge was to design an experience that made this complex technology feel simple, secure, and trustworthy for both sharers and seekers.

Seamless User Experience

We designed a frictionless flow that gets users connected in under 60 seconds, while maintaining robust security and transparency.
