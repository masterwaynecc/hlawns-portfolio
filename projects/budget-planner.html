Budget Planner Finance App

With a user-centered approach, the goal was to create an intuitive interface for effortless financial management while incorporating gamification elements to encourage healthy spending habits.

Digital Brand Assets Brand Strategy UX/UI Design
Client FinTech Startup
Timeline 12 Weeks
Year 2024

Project Overview

The Budget Planner Finance App was designed to help millennials and Gen-Z users take control of their finances through an engaging, gamified experience. The challenge was to make financial planning feel less like a chore and more like a rewarding journey.

Our research revealed that traditional finance apps often feel cold and intimidating. We set out to create something different—an app that celebrates financial wins, no matter how small, and guides users toward their goals with encouragement rather than judgment.

Design Process

We followed a comprehensive design thinking methodology, ensuring every decision was backed by user research and validated through testing.

01

Discovery

Conducted 20+ user interviews and analyzed competitor apps to understand pain points in personal finance management.

02

Ideation

Created user personas, journey maps, and explored gamification mechanics that would resonate with our target audience.

03

Design

Developed wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and a comprehensive design system for consistency across the app.

Results & Impact

The app launched to positive reception, with users praising its intuitive interface and motivating approach to financial planning.
