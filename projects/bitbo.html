Bitbo - Bitcoin Stats & Data

A comprehensive cryptocurrency analytics platform designed to make complex blockchain data accessible and actionable for both novice and experienced traders.

Research UX/UI Design Framer Development
Client Bitbo Inc.
Timeline 16 Weeks
Year 2024

The Challenge

Cryptocurrency data is notoriously complex and overwhelming. Bitbo needed a platform that could present real-time blockchain analytics, market trends, and trading signals in a way that empowers users to make informed decisions without requiring a PhD in data science.

Our mission was to transform raw data into visual insights, creating an experience that feels intuitive whether you're checking your first Bitcoin price or analyzing complex on-chain metrics.

Key Features

We designed a suite of features that cater to different user needs while maintaining a cohesive, professional aesthetic.

Real-time Charts

Interactive candlestick and line charts with customizable timeframes and technical indicators.

Price Alerts

Smart notifications that keep users informed of significant price movements and market events.

Portfolio Tracking

Comprehensive portfolio management with profit/loss calculations and historical performance.

On-chain Analytics

Deep blockchain data analysis including whale movements, exchange flows, and network health.

Impact & Results

Since launch, Bitbo has become a trusted resource for cryptocurrency enthusiasts and professional traders alike.
